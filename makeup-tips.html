Essential Makeup Tips

Here are some expert tips to elevate your makeup game:

Prepping Your Skin

  • Always start with a clean face.
  • Moisturize to hydrate your skin before applying makeup.
  • Use a primer to help makeup adhere and last longer.

Application Techniques

Using the right techniques can make all the difference:

  • Use a beauty sponge for a seamless foundation application.
  • Don’t skip setting spray to lock your look in place.
  • Always blend, blend, blend for a natural finish.

Removing Makeup

At the end of the day, it’s crucial to remove your makeup properly:


  • Use a gentle makeup remover or micellar water.

  • Follow up with your regular skincare routine.
