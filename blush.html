Choosing the Right Blush

When selecting blush, consider your skin tone:

  • Fair Skin: Soft pinks and peaches work well.
  • Medium Skin: Corals and rose tones are flattering.
  • Deep Skin: Bold colors like berry or deep plum are ideal.

Application Tips


  1. Smile to find the apples of your cheeks.
  2. Using a fluffy brush, apply blush in a circular motion.
  3. Blend upwards towards your temples for a natural look.
  4. Build coverage gradually for a more polished finish.
