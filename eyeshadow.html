Mastering Eyeshadow

Eyeshadow can enhance your eyes and add dimension to your look. Here are some essential techniques:

Color Theory

Understanding color theory is crucial for choosing complementary shades:

  • Neutral Shades: Great for a base or blending.
  • Complementary Colors: Opposite colors on the color wheel create striking contrasts.
  • Monochromatic Looks: Using different shades of the same color for a harmonious appearance.

Application Techniques



  1. Apply primer for longevity.
  2. Use a flat brush for packing color.
  3. Blend with a fluffy brush for seamless transitions.
  4. Highlight the inner corners for brightness.
