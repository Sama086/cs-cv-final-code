Types of Eyeliner

  • Pencil: Easy to use and great for beginners.
  • Liquid: Provides a bold, precise line; great for dramatic looks.
  • Gel: Offers the best of both worlds; smooth and long-lasting.
  • Marker: Convenient for quick application and control.

Application Tips

  1. Start with a clean use a primer for longevity.
  2. Begin at the inner corner and work your way out.
  3. For a winged look, extend at the outer corner.
