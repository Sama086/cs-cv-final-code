Current Makeup Trends

Stay updated with the latest makeup trends from the beauty world. Below are some of the top trends taking over this season.

1. Glass Skin

This trend, originating from Korea, focuses on achieving an ultra-dewy, luminous complexion that resembles the smooth, clear look of glass. The glass skin look is achieved with layers of hydrating products and a minimal, lightweight foundation.

  • Use hydrating serums and moisturizers.
  • Apply a light, dewy foundation or tinted moisturizer.
  • Highlight natural high points on the face for a glowing effect.

2. Bold, Colorful Eyeliner

Experimenting with bold, colorful eyeliner is a popular trend that makes the eyes pop and allows for creative expression. Whether it’s bright blue, neon green, or metallic shades, colorful liner adds a modern twist.

  • Choose shades that complement your eye color.
  • Experiment with different eyeliner shapes, like wings, graphic lines, or floating creases.
  • Pair with minimal eyeshadow for maximum impact.

3. Natural, Feathered Brows

The natural brow trend emphasizes soft, feathered brows with a slightly untamed look. This style gives a youthful, relaxed look that’s easy to maintain.

  • Use a brow gel to brush brows upward for a full effect.
  • Fill in with a brow pencil to create a natural look without overdefining.
  • Avoid heavy brow pomades for a lighter, more natural appearance.
